Is There Life After Life?

YES! There is life after life. But where you go when you die depends on your relationship with God. Right now your relationship with God is broken because of your sin. John 10:28,”and I give eternal life to them, and they will never perish…” The “them” in this verse are the people that repent and put their trust in Jesus. That is how to get forgiveness for your sin. It also says,”they will never perish.” This is talking about your soul perishing in hell. It’s also known as the second death. Just like we have two types of life, our current life and the afterlife, there are two types of death, physical death and spiritual death of your soul in hell. You need to get forgiveness for your sin and get your name written in God’s book of life. After you do that, you will no longer take part in the second death, death of your soul in hell.
