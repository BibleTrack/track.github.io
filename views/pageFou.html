Where Will You go When You Die?

I think you know, deep down inside, that your soul will live on after you die. The question is, where will you go? According to what God has told us in the Bible, there are only two places we can spend eternity, heaven or hell. Rev. 20:15, “And if any was not found written in the book of life, he was cast into the lake of fire.” When your sins are forgiven your name is written in the book of life. This allows you to escape hell, reserves your place in heaven, AND gives meaning and purpose in THIS life! This is how you get your sins forgiven. Admit your sins will cause you to go to hell. Next repent of your sins. Acts 5:19, “Repent therefore and be converted, that your sins may be blotted out.” Then call out to God. Ro. 10:13,”For everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved.”
