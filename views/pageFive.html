This is the Reason for Living!

Don’t waste anymore of your time! God wants to make you His child. What you need to do is, Pray to God, accept what Jesus did on the cross to pay for your sins and turn from your sins (repent). When you do this, God forgives your sins, makes you His child, reserves your place in heaven, and you will start enjoying His presence in your life! It is the reason He created you, so you can become His child! Now that God saved you, pray as often as you can. Read the Bible and find a good Bible teaching church to go to AND, tell others so they can go to heaven too!!! John 10:28, “and I give eternal life to them, and they will never perish;”
