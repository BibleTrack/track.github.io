What’s My Purpose in Life?

Your purpose in life and meaning in life are intertwined. You were created to be a child of God and have a relationship with Him. But, sin has destroyed that relationship so you can’t honor Him, therefore it leaves you feeling like life has no purpose. The good news is, God made a way to get rid of your sin and restore your relationship with Him! This is your purpose in life, to become God’s child and have a relationship with Him! You see, Jesus paid for your sin by dying on the cross. Ro. 5:8,”But God shows His love for us, in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us.” God requires payment for your sin. Either you will pay for it in hell for eternity or you can accept Jesus’ payment on the cross. You accept it by putting your faith in His death on the cross for the payment for your sin. This is how God forgives you of your sin.
